Our Story

Brewed with
purpose and
highland pride.

Summit Sips is more than a café — it's a movement to celebrate BENGUET agriculture, uplift mountain communities, and bring world-class specialty coffee and tea to your hands.

Our Mission

Closing the gap between farm and cup.

Most Benguet coffee and tea travels through too many hands before it reaches you — losing traceability, freshness, and the story behind it. We founded Summit Sips to dismantle that chain entirely.

We travel directly to the highlands, meet the farmers, assess the harvest, and build relationships that last seasons — not just transactions. The result is a cup you can trust, from an origin you can name.

"Tasting our coffee should feel like standing on a Benguet terrace at dawn — clear, honest, and completely alive."
— Shannen Sindayen, Co-Founder & Head Roaster

Our Journey

How Summit Sips came to be.

From a small kitchen roaster to a highland-rooted craft café — every milestone shaped the cup you hold today.

The First Bag

Co-founders Hannie and Rinri haul back 5 kg of Benguet Arabica from a weekend trip to Atok. A single pour-over in their Baguio City apartment changes everything.

2019
2020

Farm Partnerships Begin

Three Ibaloi farmer families in Benguet join as direct-trade partners. Summit Sips starts a home-roasting micro-batch subscription — 40 early subscribers become the founding community.

Sagada Tea Arrives

A chance introduction to a Sagada elder leads to sourcing wild mountain tea leaves — the first non-coffee product and a defining moment for the brand identity.

2021
2022

First Mobile Cart Launches

Summit Sips launches its first mobile cart at Irisan — starting from the heart of the Irisan community. With traceability QR codes on every cup and zero imported beans, the highlands come to you.

SCA Recognition & Expansion

Benguet Arabica lot achieves an 88-point SCA score — the highest ever recorded for a Benguet washed process. Two new farm regions join: Sultan Kudarat Robusta and Batangas Barako.

2024
2026

Online Platform Launches NOW

Summit Sips goes digital — order ahead, explore full traceability, and have your favorite Benguet-grown brew ready when you arrive. The highlands, now at your fingertips.

What We Stand For

Principles we never compromise.

These aren't marketing words. They're the commitments we make to every farmer, every customer, and every cup.

Benguet-First Sourcing

Every single ingredient we use is grown in the Benguets. We don't import to fill gaps — we build relationships to make local exceptional.

Ecological Responsibility

Shade-grown cultivation only. We reject farms that clear forest for monoculture — our highlands need biodiversity to keep producing extraordinary flavor.

Direct & Fair Trade

We pay above-market prices direct to farmers — cutting out brokers and ensuring the people who do the hardest work earn the most from it.

Full Transparency

Every cup carries a QR code linking to the farm, harvest date, elevation, and farmer name. We believe you deserve to know exactly what you're drinking.

Community Investment

5% of every sale goes directly into a community fund for farm infrastructure, children's education, and climate-resilience programs in our sourcing regions.

Uncompromising Quality

We cup every lot against SCA standards before it makes it to our menu. If it doesn't score 84+, it doesn't go in your cup — regardless of the harvest pressure.
