Craft & Method

How we brew differently.

Great ingredients deserve intentional technique. Every cup at Summit Sips follows a deliberate process built on respect for the source.

01

Precision Temperature

We dial water temperature to within 1°C of ideal for each origin — no guesswork, just science that honors the bean.

02

Timed Extraction

Bloom, pour, and pull — every stage is timed to extract sweetness without bitterness from our Benguet-grown lots.

03

Single-Origin Focus

No blending to hide flaws. We celebrate the distinct terroir of each region — earthy Barako, floral Benguet, grassy Sagada tea.

04

Mobile-First Service

Order ahead on your phone, skip the queue, and collect your cup exactly when it's ready. Convenience meets craft.

05

Traceability QR

Scan the tag on your cup to see the exact farm, harvest date, and elevation your drink came from. Full transparency.

06

Wellness Pairings

Each beverage on our menu includes a tasting profile and wellness note — so you sip with intention, not just habit.
